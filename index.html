Precoa Sales Awards

Precoa’s sales awards are designed to recognize those who make a difference each day.

They showcase the efforts of our highest achievers while giving everyone an opportunity to reach the next milestone with the support of their peers.

Through efforts large and small, we’re changing our profession for the better. And together, we rise by lifting others, helping each other to be the best we can be.

Portland MDP Event

Each summer, the newest Million Dollar Producers (and their guest) from the previous year will be invited to visit Precoa headquarters and enjoy beautiful Portland, Oregon. During their time in the Rose City, they will get a behind the scenes tour and be recognized of their achievement.

All physical awards will be mailed following the annual Precoa Escapes incentive trip.
